Show available CM templates

In the OpenEMM navigation bar, click on Mailings and Content manager to have a list of available CM templates output.

As for the module types and the content modules, the overview table uses lists with a practical preview function. OpenEMM shows each CM template in a small preview window that informs you of its contents at a glance. This is particularly useful when you work with many different CM templates and want to access a specific template quickly.

Fig. 9.16: OpenEMM displays a list of all available CM templates and a preview image.

The left-hand column contains the name of the individual CM template and in the middle the preview image. You can edit the CM template using the symbols in the right-hand column of the table or by clicking the name.
