Deleting a template

If required, a template can be deleted again. A template can only be deleted if it has been saved first. You can now do one of two things:

•By clicking on Mailings and Templates in the navigation bar, open the list of all available templates. Click on the Delete symbol behind the required template.
•By clicking on Mailings and Templates in the navigation bar, open the list of all available templates. By clicking on the name of the required template, open the entry dialog for that template. At the bottom of the website, click on die Delete button.

In both cases, OpenEMM will ask you to answer a confirmation query to confirm that you do, indeed, wish to delete the template. Just click on the Delete button. If you click on Cancel instead, the template will not be deleted.
