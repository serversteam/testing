What are the available statistical functions?

The Statistics section supplies you with more detailed information about customer behavior following the mailing. The OpenEMM system gives you far-ranging evaluation options which enable you to track click rates for links sent within e-mails. You can not only display which links are clicked on and how often, but also check the overall status of your subscribers. Very precise measuring instruments are at your disposal, from the basic statistical functions to detailed recipient statistics.

One special feature are archive statistics. An archive groups several mailings. Archive statistics displays an overview analysis for each mailing grouped within that campaign as well as a total value. Further information on archive statistics is contained in chapter Statistical evaluation.
