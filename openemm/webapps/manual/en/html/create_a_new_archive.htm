Create a new archive

To create a new archive, go to the Mailings section, Archive subsection in the navigation bar.

1.Open the Archive section and click on the tab New archive.
2.The entry dialog calls up the Name field where you can enter a short archive description, e.g. Newsletter. The Description should consist of one or two sentences characterizing your new archive. This will make mailing management easier once you have many archive which are used by several staff.
3.Click on Save to conclude your entries and close the field.

Fig. 3.55: All a new archive needs is two entries.

The OpenEMM will now display the overview listing all existing archive, including the one you just created.
